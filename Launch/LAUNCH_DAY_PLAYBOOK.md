## Crisis Management

### If App Crashes on Launch Day

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **Acknowledge publicly:**
   ```
   We're aware of crash issues affecting some users.
   Our team is investigating and will have a fix shortly.
   Thank you for your patience.
   ```

2. **Fix urgently:**
   - Identify cause
   - Create hotfix
   - Test thoroughly
   - Upload to App Store Connect

3. **Submit for expedited review:**
   - Mark as critical bug fix
   - Explain situation in notes
   - Request expedited review

4. **Communicate progress:**
   - Update every 2 hours
   - Be transparent
   - Apologize sincerely

5. **Compensate affected users:**
   - Offer free Pro upgrade
   - Extend trial period
   - Personal apology email

### If Bad Review Appears

**Response Strategy:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Be professional and empathetic
- Offer to help resolve issue
- Take conversation to email if needed

**Example Response:**
```
Hi [Reviewer Name],

Thank you for your feedback. I'm sorry QuoteHub
didn't meet your expectations.

I'd love to understand what went wrong and make
it right. Could you email me at support@quotehub.app
so I can help?

[Specific response to their concern]

We're committed to making QuoteHub better every day.

[Your Name], Founder
```

### If Press Coverage is Negative

**Response Plan:**
1. Don't respond emotionally
2. Take 24 hours to craft response
3. Address legitimate concerns
4. Provide clarifications if needed
5. Turn criticism into improvement opportunity
