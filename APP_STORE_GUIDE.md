Required keys:
```xml

<string>1.0.0</string>

<string>¬© 2026 Lakshami Contractors. All rights reserved.</string>
```

### 3. App Signing

1. In Xcode, select project
2. Signing & Capabilities tab:
   - **Team**: Select your Apple Developer account
   - **Signing Certificate**: Apple Distribution
   - **Automatically manage signing**: Enabled (recommended)

### 4. Entitlements

Required entitlements (already in macappyessir.entitlements):
- App Sandbox: Enabled
- User Selected Files: Read/Write (for PDF export)
- Camera Access (for photo capture)
- File System: User Selected Files

### 5. Archive the App

1. In Xcode: Product ‚Üí Archive
2. Wait for archive to complete
3. Organizer window opens automatically

### 6. Upload to App Store Connect

1. In Organizer, select your archive
2. Click "Distribute App"
3. Select "App Store Connect"
4. Click "Next"
5. Select "Upload"
6. Review entitlements and signing
7. Click "Upload"
8. Wait for upload to complete (~5-10 minutes)

- **Solution**: Test all advertised features work

- **Solution**: Keep all content professional and appropriate
